Uzox Commands


All the commands you need to know to keep Uzox running in your server.

Music Control Commands

PLAY (P)

Play songs from YouTube, SoundCloud or Spotify using their names or links!

SKIP (S)

Skip the song playing currently.

PAUSE (PS)

Pause the song playing currently.

RESUME (R)

Resume the song playing currently.

VOLUME (V)

Check or change the volume of the song playing currently.

SEARCH (SRCH)

Searches YouTube for the argument passed.

NOWPLAYING (NP)

Check what's playing.

PLAYLIST (PL)

Search YouTube for a playlist with the name.

LYRICS (LY)

Get the lyrics of the currently playing song.

PLAYNEXT (PN)

Play songs from YouTube, SoundCloud or Spotify using their names or links next.

Queue Control Commands

QUEUE (Q)

Show the queue playing of the server.

STOP (DC)

Stops the queue and leaves the voice channel.

SHUFFLE (SH)

Shuffle the queue.

REMOVE (RM)

Remove a song from the queue using the queue number

SKIPTO (ST)

Skip to a song from the queue.

LOOP (L)

Loops the currently playing queue.

MOVE

Move a song from a position in the queue to another.

Invite Commands

INVITE (INV)

Generate an invite link for the server valid for 1 day.

BOTINV (BINV)

Generate a bot invite link to invite the bot to your server.

Moderation Commands

KICK

Kick a member from the server.

BAN

Ban a member from the server.

UNBAN

Unban a member from the server.

PURGE (PRG)

Purge a maximum of 100 messages

COOLDOWN (CD)

Set the cooldown of a channel.

Favourites/Personal Playlists Commands

FAV ADD

Add songs into your fav list(First time creates a list)

FAV REMOVE

Remove songs from your fav list.

FAV PLAY (P)

Play a song from you fav list directly.

FAV PLAYLIST (PL)

Play multiple songs from your fav list directly.

FAV LIST

Check your fav list.

Music Filter Commands

FILTER [NAME]


Use a filter from the following:
bassboost, 8D, vaporwave, nightcore, phaser, tremolo, vibrato, reverse, treble, normalizer, surround, pulsator, subboost, karaoke, flanger, gate, haas and mcompand.

FILTER CLEAR

Clear all the filters on the current playing song.

DJ Commands

SETDJ

Automatically finds the DJ role and enables it.

FORCESKIP (FS)

Bypasses the vote system for skipping.

Fun Commands

MEME (M)

Generate a random meme.

AVATAR (AV)

Show the avatar of a user.

SERVERAV (SAV)

Show the server icon.

AWW

Generate a random cute image.

GIF

Generate a random GIF from tenor.

8BALL

Let Uzox take your decisions or answer.

FACT

Generate a random fact.

RIDDLE

Let Uzox ask you a riddle.

QUOTE

Take inspiration from good quotes.

Move a song from a position in the queue to another.
